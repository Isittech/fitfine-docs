# ℹ️ About FitFine

**FitFine** is a health and fitness companion app designed to help users track calories, workouts, water intake, steps, and general wellness goals through simple tools and AI-assisted suggestions.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

FitFine helps users organize their fitness journey for goals such as **weight loss**, **muscle gain**, or **weight maintenance** using user-provided information and estimated calculations.

All suggestions are provided for **general informational and motivational purposes only**.

---

## 🔍 Key Features

### 🧮 1. Health Calculators
- Estimates **Body Mass Index (BMI)** and **daily calorie needs**
- Uses user input such as **height, weight, age, gender**, and **goal**
- Results are estimates and may vary from person to person

### 🥗 2. Diet Suggestions
- Provides general meal and diet suggestions based on calorie goals and user preferences
- Food and calorie information may be based on public nutrition references, available databases such as **USDA FoodData Central**, AI-assisted outputs, and manual review
- Calorie and nutrition values are estimates and may vary depending on food type, quantity, preparation, and data source

### 🏋️ 3. Workout Suggestions
- Provides general workout suggestions based on fitness goals and experience level
- May include strength, cardio, yoga, and flexibility routines
- Workout suggestions are not a substitute for certified fitness coaching or medical advice

---

## ⚙️ Supporting Tools

### 💧 Water Tracker  
Helps users track daily water intake.

### 👣 Step Counter  
Uses device sensors where available to estimate steps. Step counts may vary depending on device, permissions, and sensor accuracy.

### 🍱 Calorie Tracker  
Helps users log food intake and estimate daily calories.

---

## 🤖 AI & Technology

FitFine may use **ChatGPT**, **Google AI**, and other tools to help generate:

- Diet suggestions
- Workout suggestions
- Calorie-related suggestions
- In-app personalization

AI-generated content may be incomplete, inaccurate, or unsuitable for some users. All AI-generated content is intended for **general informational use only** and should not be treated as medical, nutrition, dietitian, or professional fitness advice.

---

## 🔐 Data Storage & Privacy

- **Firebase** may be used for authentication, database storage, analytics, crash reporting, and app performance.
- **Google AdMob** may be used to display ads.
- Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on user settings, consent, and Google AdMob policies.
- FitFine does not sell personal data.

Please read our Privacy Policy for details about data collection, usage, retention, deletion, and third-party services.

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

- FitFine does **not** provide medical advice, professional nutrition advice, dietitian services, or certified fitness coaching.
- Always consult a qualified healthcare provider, registered dietitian, nutritionist, or certified fitness professional before making significant changes to diet, exercise, supplements, weight-loss plans, or lifestyle.
- Results are not guaranteed and may vary from user to user.
- BMI, calorie, workout, water, nutrition, and step values are **estimates** for **informational and motivational purposes only**.
