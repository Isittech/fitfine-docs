# 🔐 Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Fitfine collects, uses, stores, and protects user data.

Fitfine respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

## Information We Collect

Fitfine may collect the following information when you use the app:

- Name
- Email address
- Account login information
- Height, weight, age, gender, and fitness preferences
- Diet, workout, calorie, nutrition, and activity-related information
- App usage data, crash logs, device information, and analytics data

We collect this information to provide app features such as calorie tracking, diet suggestions, workout plans, progress tracking, account management, app performance improvement, and personalized user experience.

## Firebase Usage

We use Firebase services, including Authentication, Firestore, Crashlytics, and Analytics.

Firebase may collect and process information such as account details, device information, app activity, crash reports, analytics data, and other technical data required to operate and improve the app.

Please refer to Firebase’s privacy policy:  
🔗 https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## AI Tools

Some features in this app, such as personalized workout, calorie, diet, or nutrition suggestions, may be powered by AI tools including ChatGPT and Google AI.

The app may use user-provided fitness, diet, workout, calorie, height, weight, and preference data to generate personalized suggestions.

These suggestions are generated automatically and should not be considered medical, fitness, nutritional, or professional advice. Users should use their own judgment and consult a qualified professional where needed.

## Advertisements

This app displays banner and interstitial ads, which may use cookies, advertising IDs, device information, or similar identifiers to provide personalized or non-personalized advertising.

By using the app, you consent to these ad services. Learn more:  
🔗 https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

## How We Use Data

We use collected data to:

- Create and manage user accounts
- Provide calorie, diet, nutrition, and workout-related features
- Personalize fitness and diet suggestions
- Track user progress
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Monitor crashes, errors, and security issues
- Display advertisements
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell your personal data.

We may share or process data with trusted third-party service providers only when required to operate app features, including:

- Firebase and Google services for authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting, and app performance
- AI service providers for generating personalized diet, workout, calorie, or fitness suggestions
- Advertising providers for showing ads
- Legal authorities if required by law

## Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure.

However, no online service or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide and improve Fitfine services, maintain app functionality, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud or abuse, and enforce our agreements.

Account information, profile details, fitness data, height, weight, diet information, workout information, calorie records, and app-related data may be retained while your account is active.

If you delete your account or request deletion of your data, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, unless we are legally required to retain certain information for a longer period.

Crash logs, analytics data, and advertising-related data may be retained by third-party services such as Firebase, Google Analytics, and advertising providers according to their own privacy and retention policies.

Some non-personal, aggregated, or anonymized data may be retained for analytics, security, and app improvement purposes, but it cannot be used to identify you.
