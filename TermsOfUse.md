 ℹ️ About FitFine

FitFine is a health and fitness companion app designed to help users track calories, workouts, water intake, steps, and general wellness goals.

FitFine provides tools and suggestions based on user inputs such as height, weight, age, gender, fitness goals, diet preferences, and activity information.

---

 🚀 Key Features

- ✅ BMI & Calorie Calculator  
  Estimates Body Mass Index and daily calorie needs using basic information such as height, weight, age, and gender.

- 🍽️ Diet Suggestions  
  Provides general diet and meal suggestions based on user preferences, calorie goals, and available food information.

- 🏋️‍♂️ Workout Suggestions  
  Provides general workout suggestions based on goals such as fat loss, muscle gain, or general fitness.

- 💧 Water Intake Tracker  
  Helps users track daily water intake.

- 👟 Step Counter  
  Uses device sensors where available to estimate steps. Step counts may vary depending on device, permissions, and sensor accuracy.

- 🔥 Calorie Tracker  
  Helps users estimate food calories and track daily intake. Calorie values are estimates and may vary depending on food type, quantity, preparation, and data source.
